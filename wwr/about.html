History

Rapid Adventures was born in 2005 when founder Jake Williams took friends down the Colorado River in a single, well-worn raft. What began as weekend adventures soon grew into a passion project, with Jake guiding trips every summer while working construction during off-seasons.

The turning point came in 2012 when National Geographic featured one of our expeditions, bringing international attention to our unique approach that blended extreme sports with environmental education. That year we expanded operations to three rivers and hired our first full-time guides.
